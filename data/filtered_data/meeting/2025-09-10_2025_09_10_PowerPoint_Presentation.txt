Appointment (action item)
Proposal: Put forth for consideration by Committee member.
Temperature check: Each Committee member indicates how
comfortable they are with making an affirmative decision.
Modified
Discussion: Additional discussion if needed.
consensus
Amendments: Committee members can offer amendments to
the original proposal.
decision
Decision: Each Committee member can 1) affirm the proposal,
2) stand aside, or 3) indicate that “no” they do not support the
making
proposal. Note that standing aside is counted as a decision to
affirm for the purposes of approving a proposal.
process
The following minimum number of affirmative decisions is required for a
decision to represent the position of the PCEF Committee:
• When 6 or 7 Committee members are present: 5 Affirmative decisions.
• When 8 or 9 Committee members are present: 6 Affirmative decisions.
