renewable energy may not be enough

Oregon and Washington are falling so far behind on upgrades to the electrical grid that

year, according to a preliminary analysis shared at a utility industry and regional power

For Oregon and Washington, the gap between how much electricity will be needed in

Starting next year, that could lead to planned and controlled shutdowns of power by

low wind and solar production.
